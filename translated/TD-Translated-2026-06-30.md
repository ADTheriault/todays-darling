## Title: Surely, "thank goodness! Thank goodness!"
## Date: 2026-06-30
## Author: Shigesato Itoi
## Content:

Once again, I'm writing this a little earlier than usual. For the soccer, of course. Kickoff is at 2 a.m., and I'm normally still awake at that hour anyway. In that sense, staying up isn't even a question. It's right in my sweet spot. But that alone doesn't solve the problem. Staying awake is easy enough, but if I'm glued to a soccer match until four in the morning, there's no time left to write this column. So I had a choice: either work myself into a fervor and write after 4 a.m., or get the column done before the match starts. One or the other.

This column might look like breezy, throwaway writing, but it's never as easy as it seems once you actually sit down to do it. Sometimes I'll try dashing one off in twenty minutes, just to see if I can speed-write it, but I end up flustered, and I suppose I just don't have the natural talent for it, so I give up with nothing to show. I'd rather not struggle with anything besides soccer in the early morning hours, so I decided to write this in advance.

Once I'd made that decision, I figured I might as well take my bath early too. Lately I've been trying to bathe early and get to bed early. It's not as easy as it sounds, but I'm doing my best. So here I am, already bathed before 10 p.m. My body is nice and warm. And since I want to be ready to sleep the moment I feel like it, I've already brushed my teeth and put on my pajamas. I check the clock: 11:17 p.m. And this column, as you can see, is already about eighty percent done.

All I had to do was grumble and gripe a little, and the thing practically wrote itself. How convenient is that? I can't exactly pull this trick every day, but this time, it really saved me.

Come to think of it, this column goes up at eleven. So the result of "that match" will be out for all to see by then, like it or not.

Thank you for visiting Hobonichi again today. Who knew a mere ten-hour time difference could hold so much depth.
