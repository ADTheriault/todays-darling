## Title: Isn't there anyone who looks happy?
## Date: 2026-05-24
## Author: Shigesato Itoi
## Content:

Is there someone you look at and think, "That person seems so happy"? It could be someone close to you, or even yourself, or some famous person you've never met. Better if they're not fictional, though. And ideally, someone you could actually go and see if you wanted to. What I'm saying is, I'd love to meet one of those people, someone others look at and think, "They seem so happy," and ask them all sorts of things. I haven't worked out any of the details yet. It just came to me.

Yesterday I went to see *The Devil Wears Prada 2*. The movie was good, really entertaining, and clearly a hit. The original, now over twenty years old, is still fun to watch, and pulling off a sequel with the same cast is practically an acrobatic feat. The fact that they managed to put together a film this solid is genuinely impressive. It's not all glamour and lightness, but the dazzling, stylish costumes do their work beautifully.

On the walk home, I was thinking to myself. Why is it that this movie doesn't feel like a happy ending? Every character is trying hard, or being smart, but to me, none of them looked happy. Including the main character. They win, they succeed, and yet there wasn't a single person on screen who struck me as someone I'd describe as "happy."

So then I turned the question on myself. The people I look at and think, "They seem so happy," where are they? Or the people everyone would point to and say, "Now there's someone who's happy," who are they, and where are they living? Do we think that about presidents, or pop stars, or athletes? Among the people I actually know, who would I say that about?

Happiness is subjective in the first place, I suppose, and the more I think about it the more tangled everything gets. But if that's the case, what if I found people who others perceive as happy, and sat down and talked to them about all kinds of things? It's still just an idea I've had on my own, but I'm planning to bring it to everyone and see what they think.

Thank you for coming to Hobonichi today. I wonder how many treatises on happiness have been written over the course of human history.
