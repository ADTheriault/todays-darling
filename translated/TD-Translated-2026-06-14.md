## Title: "Nobody special."
## Date: 2026-06-14
## Author: Shigesato Itoi
## Content:

Being truly "nothing special." Being "nothing special" from the bottom of your heart. Your foolishness plain for everyone to see, everyone knowing full well you're "nothing special." You can try your best to bluff. You can put on the grandest airs you've got. But "nothing special" is "nothing special."

You might be making your own quiet efforts, struggling in ways nobody ever notices, but "nothing special" is still "nothing special." You might have a little kindness in you, you might manage a good deed now and then, but "nothing special" is, no matter what, "nothing special."

Win a gold medal, still "nothing special." Get called a great person, still "nothing special." Whatever you do, whatever you're capable of, you were "nothing special" to begin with, so "nothing special" you remain.

Me, you, him, her, all of us, "nothing special" from the day we were born. Once in a while we're a little "better." Once in a while we get a little "serious." But being "nothing special" doesn't mean you lose anything, doesn't put you at any disadvantage.

As "nothing special," you can still climb the highest mountains, dive into the deepest seas. You can stay "nothing special" and still laugh, cry, feel joy, find happiness, even pull off something incredible.

"Nothing special," and no complaints about it.

Thank you for coming to Hobonichi again today. There probably isn't a single person alive who is anything other than "nothing special."
